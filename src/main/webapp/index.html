welcome

This website has goal to help you learning new vocabulary from foreign language.

After adding the translations you could create your list of word to learn. Then run quizz to get score.

To use the it:

  • register, use a fake email, because it's useless now
  • add your language you need
  • add the translation you need between two language
  • add the learning words you need from a language to another
  • start quizz when you want

Words are removed from quizz when you answer almost 50 times with a score of 75%.

If you see a wrong translation you can click on it during the quizz answer, after a certain amont of click it will be removed.
